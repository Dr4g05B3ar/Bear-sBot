Command List

Moderation Commands

Purge; purges messages in the chat, Manage Messages permissions are required; BB!purge [Num 1-100]

Timeout; Times out a member for a certain amount of time, Moderate Members permissions are required; BB!time [Member]

Untime; Untimes a member, Moderate Members permissions are required; BB!untime [Member]

Kick; Kicks a member from the server, kick permissions are required; BB!kick [Member]

Ban; Bans a member from the server, ban permissions are required; BB!ban [Member]

Fun Commands

Hang Man; start a game of hangman, BB!hm

Higher or Lower; start a game of higher or lower, BB!hl

Guess Higher or Lower; guess a number for your game, BB!ghl

Roll; roll a number starting at 1, BB!roll [Highest Number]

Rock Paper Scissors; start a game of rock paper scissors, BB!rps

Trivia; start a game of trivia, BB!s-t

Wheel of Names; spin for a name, BB!wn [Names seperated by ONLY a space]

Rizz; get a rizz line, BB!rizz (Warning some might be NSFW)
