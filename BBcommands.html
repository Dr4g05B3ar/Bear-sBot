Command List

Moderation Commands

Purge; purges messages in the chat, Manage Messages permissions are required; BB!purge [Num 1-100]

Timeout; Times out a member for a certain amount of time, Moderate Members permissions are required; BB!time [Member]

Untime; Untimes a member, Moderate Members permissions are required; BB!untime [Member]

Kick; Kicks a member from the server, kick permissions are required; BB!kick [Member]

Ban; Bans a member from the server, ban permissions are required; BB!ban [Member]
